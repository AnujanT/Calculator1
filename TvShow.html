Physical Characteristics

House Baratheon - Black hair, blue eyes, and square jaws. House Bolton - Black hair and eerie "ghost grey" eyes. House Florent - Large ears. House Lannister - Curly blonde hair and green eyes. House Martell - Classic "Salty Dornish" features of olive skin, dark hair, and dark eyes. House Redwyne - Red Hair. House Stark - Black Hair and Grey eyes. House Umber - Very large size, frequently over six feet tall and well-built. House Targaryen - Classic Valyrian features of pale skin, white platinum-blonde hair. House Tully - Auburn hair and blue eyes.
